Work-Speech draft

发表于2023-11-28|更新于2023-11-29|Work

Dear listeners,

Today, I’m proud to introduce you to lululemon athletica, a trendsetting, healthy-lifestyle company. The company is not just a leading brand of yoga clothing, it is a representative of an attitude towards life.

（公司）

Lululemon was founded in 1998 as a design studio that provided high-quality yoga equipment to yoga enthusiasts. Over time, lululemon has evolved into a global company headquartered in Vancouver, Canada. Its founder is Chip Wilson, an entrepreneur who loves sports and healthy living. Under his leadership, lululemon continues to innovate and is committed to providing the best options for consumers looking for functional and stylish sports equipment. In 2004, the company’s sales were only 18 million US dollars, and in 2007, it was listed on Nasdaq. In 2017, its revenue reached 2.6 billion US dollars. In 2021, it had more than 500 stores, and the pingefficiency once exceeded 20,000 US dollars/square meter, second only to Apple in retail stores.

When lululemon was founded, its stores were design workshops during the day and yoga studios at night. It was also this model that made lululemon gain a large number of loyal customers in the early days and officially opened its first store in 2000. In 2013, lululemon was ready to enter the domestic market, but it did not open a store first, but in Shanghai and Beijing in the form of “Show Room” to contact with domestic consumers. It wasn’t until 2016 that lululemon officially opened physical stores in China. And the purchasing power of the Chinese people is well known, with retail sales in the country outperforming those in North America. Early lululemon did not rush to open a store, but cast a net online with the help of e-commerce. Before the epidemic began to expand the scale of offline stores, but since the epidemic stores have also closed a lot, at the end of this year, lululemon in China has 71 stores.

As a popular brand worldwide, lululemon distributes its products through online stores and physical stores around the world. Each store is not only a retail space, but also a platform to connect the community and share the fun of yoga and sports. From the perspective of business characteristics, Lululemon is more of a combination of artisan brands and lifestyle brands. In terms of products, Lululemon’s product development and design bring the design concept of “feeling science” to the extreme, bring the advantages of technology and fabric to the extreme, and carefully design around the scene combining comfort and function; Attach importance to technological innovation and integrate fashion elements.

（产品）

Next, I’m going to dive into our protagonist of the day, lululemon’s yoga wear, and how it plays an integral role in our lives.

Before diving into lululemon yoga wear, let’s pause for a moment and imagine the perfect yoga experience. Yes, it should be free and fluid, full of inner harmony and peace. lululemon’s yoga wear aims to provide exactly that experience. First of all, we would like to mention its unparalleled comfort. Crafted from cutting-edge textiles like Luon and Luxtreme, these yoga apparels offer multidirectional stretch, seamlessly conforming to every contour of your physique, enhancing fluidity in yoga poses without any restriction to your innate range of motion. Engineered for superior moisture management, these materials excel in drawing perspiration away from the skin, ensuring comfort and dryness through vigorous yoga sessions.

Lululemon’s yoga apparel also stands out for its enduring quality. Crafted for longevity, these pieces resist fading and maintain their form through numerous washes. Essentially, when you purchase a yoga outfit from lululemon, you’re securing a lasting partner for your fitness journey.

Moreover, the brand’s fashion-forward approach is evident. Recognizing that today’s yoga practitioners demand attire that’s as chic as it is practical, the designers at lululemon tirelessly innovate. They offer an array of yoga apparel in diverse styles and hues to cater to the unique preferences and tastes of each individual.

Versatility is another hallmark of lululemon’s yoga attire. Far beyond the confines of the yoga studio, these garments are impeccably suited for the great outdoors, jogging paths, or simply for everyday flair. Whether it’s a leisurely day shopping or a casual meetup with friends, lululemon ensures you step out in style.

To encapsulate, lululemon yoga attire epitomizes an ideal fusion: it marries innovation with style, melds comfort with resilience, and harmonizes practicality with diversity. It’s for these reasons that a myriad of yoga devotees consistently turn to lululemon as their preferred brand.

（客户画像）

Lululemon’s customer base is diverse, but there are some common characteristics among them.

Lululemon’s founder once described his ideal customer as “a 32-year-old professional single woman named Ocean making $100,000 a year.” Ocean is engaged, has her own apartment, travels, is fashion-conscious, and exercises for an hour and a half every day.” This is a typical customer of lululemon, and it can also dig up a portrait of its target consumers. On the whole, Lululemon’s customers are consumers with higher education, higher income level and focus on quality of life, mainly young women and celebrities. These consumers pay attention to brand image and fitness experience, like to exercise in high-end fitness centers, and are willing to pay high prices for high-quality fitness clothes and accessories.

Demographically, lululemon’s customers are typically middle - and upper-income, ranging in age from 20 to 40, who are health-conscious, active and willing to invest in a high-quality lifestyle. While lululemon began as a predominantly female customer, it now has a growing male customer base as well. Although lululemon is developing male customer groups, according to the data of similarweb, women account for 70.8% of its audience, and men account for 29.2%, with a large gender difference, women are still the main audience and consumers of lululemon.

Geographically, these customers are spread across the globe. Whether it is the urban centers of North America, the modern cities of Asia, or the fashion capitals of Europe, lululemon yoga wear is the ideal choice for those who are looking for stylish and functional exercise equipment.

Psychologically, lululemon customers are often those who pursue self-realization and pay attention to physical and mental balance. They may be yoga enthusiasts, fitness fanatics, or just fashion-conscious people looking for comfortable everyday clothes. These customers tend to focus on quality, seek products that are different, and are willing to pay more for them.

Regarding their activities and social habits, those who patronize lululemon often find themselves regularly attending yoga sessions and gym-related events, take pleasure in outdoor sporting pursuits, and exhibit sociability with a propensity to disseminate their pursuit of a healthful way of living among peers. Their allegiance to the lululemon brand is evident as they frequently advocate for the brand’s wares via word of mouth and on digital social platforms.

As for the fidelity to the brand, patrons of lululemon are typically habitual purchasers, possessing a profound comprehension of the distinct worth the brand offers, and resonate deeply with the life ethos advocated by lululemon. These are patrons who navigate hectic lifestyles and consistently eschew anything less than superior quality.

In summation, the clientele of lululemon embodies the zeitgeist of modernity, characterized by an active stance, a flair for style, and stringent standards for an enhanced life quality. Their purchasing decisions transcend mere transactions for items—they invest in a way of life that reflects and upholds their individual principles.

（感知图描述）

To gain a profound insight into the strategic market placement of lululemon, let’s employ the perceptual map, a graphical representation that locates brands on a matrix based on the consumer’s perspective, aligning them with critical attributes. Within this framework, lululemon’s spot is determined primarily by two axes: the vertical representing price and the horizontal showcasing product quality along with innovation.

Positioning on the perceptual map places the price on the y-axis, while the x-axis is home to quality and innovation. Here, lululemon finds itself in the top right quadrant, signifying the brand’s high-end pricing and consumer recognition of its superior quality and cutting-edge innovation.

lululemon differentiates itself from the competition with its exclusive fabric technologies, innovative patents, and its focus on a health-conscious lifestyle, which is evident in its quality and innovative edge. This is reflected in a pricing approach that aligns with the upscale nature of the brand, appealing to customers who value quality and are willing to invest in brand worth.

This perceptual mapping illustrates the stark distinction between lululemon and brands that compete in the economy sector, offering products at lower prices with moderate quality. lululemon’s strategy eschews the race to the bottom in pricing, opting instead to deliver unparalleled value, cementing its distinctive standing in the consumer psyche.

（分销渠道）

Now, let’s shift our focus to the avenues through which lululemon Yoga apparel reaches its patrons, a crucial aspect for comprehending the company’s operational model. More than just a creator of premium yoga attire, lululemon meticulously orchestrates its distribution channels to guarantee that wherever they are, consumers have the ease of access to purchase and immerse themselves in what the brand offers.

Foremost, the global landscape is dotted with lululemon’s brick-and-mortar locations. These establishments transcend mere transaction points; they embody the ethos of the brand and foster a communal bond. Patrons here are privy to firsthand product encounters, personalized guidance from in-store experts, and insights on optimizing product utility. Such a tangible shopping venture is the cornerstone of lululemon’s distribution blueprint.

Equally pivotal is the brand’s digital footprint. Via its virtual storefront, lululemon lays out an expansive digital marketplace that beckons customers to effortlessly peruse and procure an array of yoga apparel and ancillaries. This digital domain prides itself on user-friendly navigation, making sure that a streamlined shopping journey is a click away for even those with the most demanding schedules.

Beyond its own digital and physical outlets, lululemon extends its reach through selective partnerships with other e-commerce platforms and niche retailers in the yoga and fitness sphere, aligning only with entities that uphold the high-caliber and ethos parallel to lululemon’s.

Furthermore, in strategic locales, lululemon advances its community-centric marketing, drawing in consumers by spearheading yoga and fitness gatherings, and forging alliances with regional wellness mentors and yogis. These initiatives serve to elevate the brand’s profile and fortify ties with its intended demographic. For example, in some communities in North America, Lululemon promotes the idea of “hot sweat living.” On the Upper East Side of the United States, Lululemon shopping bags are almost everywhere, printed with inspirational words such as “Do one thing every day to surprise yourself” and so on. In Westchester, Lululemon seems to have become a social tool, and many people wear Lululemon to find common topics and become friends.

In short, lululemon’s distribution channels are diversified and well-planned, and it effectively brings its products to customers around the world through physical stores, online platforms and community activities, while strengthening its brand image and market position.

（广告词）

Let lululemon yoga wear accompany you to every stretch and breath as you start your day.

Experience the perfect fusion of design and comfort, and feel the freedom of every movement.

Our patented Luon fabric, stretched in four directions, supports your every pose, whether on the yoga mat or in every moment of life.

No matter where you are, lululemon yoga wear ADAPTS to your energetic lifestyle, bringing grace and power to your daily routine.

At lululemon, we don’t just offer clothes, we offer an attitude to life.

lululemon - Unlock your potential and live your true self.

Visit our website or your nearest lululemon store to start your yoga journey today.

（结语）

Lululemon’s story is not just about its products, but how it impacts and inspires each of us, encouraging us to live healthier and more confident lives. Each of its products carries the potential to make our daily lives better. Finally, thank you very much for listening and participating today.

Thank you very much.
